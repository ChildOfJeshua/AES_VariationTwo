Our Story:

Who We Are

AES Literacy Institute was founded by Mr. Jermaine & Mrs. ShaKimberly Cooper. A husband-and-wife team dedicated to motivating underpriviledged adult learners to obtain the education and career training necessary to be self-sufficient.

Coming from an environment of social and economic challenges similarly faced by many AES Learners, our sense of of motivation is rooted in the energy, persistancy, and deep connection to the people we serve. Our goal is to enable whomever joins any of our programs, whether it be educational systems, building businesses, financial literacy courses and so much more, to over achieve in their aspirations.

Lastly, We believe people don’t lack potential; they lack access. So with that said, there is no required roadmap as the whole idea is to encourage a more natural curiosity towards learning what more they may feel drawn towards.
