OUR MISSION STATEMENT

"Our mission is to motivate underpriviledged adult learners to obtain the education and career training necessary to be self-sufficient. By reducing barriers and increasing access to career opportunities we will help restore diginity and improve quality of life," AES Institute.

EDUCATE

We believe that being successful beings with a strong educational foundation. Adults that often lack a high school diploma or its equivalency are 10x more likely to suffer from poverty and chromic unemployment. AES aims to ensure every individual has the educational foundation needed to excel in life.

  • Tools Such as:
    • Faith-centered approach
    • Creative excellence
    • Community support

MOTIVATE

Motivation is a powerful tool. Everything begins from within, what a person believes rather than he or she is. AES aims to motivate individuals who feel that all hope is gone. We work to develop positive thinking mindsets for the purpose of wiping out self-doubt and negativity so that individuals can achieve their dreams!

  • Tools Such as:
    • Faith-centered approach
    • Creative excellence
    • Community support

ELEVATE

By combining motivation, education, career training, and practical skills, we can restore hope and diginity within an individual. By connecting adult learners with the many educational and career reosurces avaliable, individuals will be able to further their education, get a job, and or enter the military.

  • Tools Such as:
    • Faith-centered approach
    • Creative excellence
    • Community support
